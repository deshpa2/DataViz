Line Graph

Viewing the line graph, we start by studying the trend of the number of complaints filed against police officers since 1985 in all of New York city. The higher the bar for a particular year, the higher is the number of complaints lodged. We can see that the complaints registered started rising after 1999 and is at peak in 2016.





Map of the New York city.

As we proceed, in this visualization, we use a map of New York city Precinct to display information about the total number of allegations made by people of different ethnicities against the New York city police department with regard to different precincts.

Sankey Graph

In addition to this we could continue forward with a detailed mapping of the allegations against the ethnicities of different officers with respect to the complainant ethnicities. This is represented by a sankey graph wherein, the thickness of bar represents the volume of allegations made against the officer of that particular ethnicity or the allegations made by specific ethnicity of complainant.





Stacked area bar chart

As we saw in the above sankey graph, we see that higher complaints have been made by people of black community. Furthermore, we wanted to show what the allegations were that the people with black ethnicity reported against the white officers. We see that most reports were for Citizen Interactions.
