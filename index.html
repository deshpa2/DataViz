Bar Chart

Viewing the Bar chart, we start by studying the trend of the number of complaints filed against police officers since 1985 in all of New York city. The higher the bar for a particular year, the higher is the number of complaints lodged. We can see that the complaints registered started rising after 1999 and is at peak in 2016. To view the precise information about the number of complaints for a particular year the user could hover over each bar.





Map of the New York city.

As we proceed, in this visualization, we use a map of New York city Precinct to display information about the total number of allegations made by people of different ethnicities against the New York city police department with regard to different precincts. In order to get precinct number and the actual count of the allegations reported, user could hover over the map.





Sankey Graph

In addition to this we could continue forward with a detailed mapping of the allegations against the ethnicities of different officers with respect to the complainant ethnicities. This is represented by a sankey graph wherein, the thickness of bar represents the volume of allegations made against the officer of that particular ethnicity or the allegations made by specific ethnicity of complainant. To view the accurate mapping between the ethnicities, the user could hover over the left and rightmost colored bars.
